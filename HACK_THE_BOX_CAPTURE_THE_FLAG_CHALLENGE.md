## Overview
This document serves as a guide for the HACK THE BOX Capture the Flag (CTF) challenge.

## Challenge Details
- **Objective:** Capture flags by exploiting vulnerabilities within the challenges provided by Hack The Box.
- **Required Skills:** Basic knowledge of penetration testing, web security, and cryptography.

## Steps to Complete the Challenge
1. **Register on Hack The Box:** Create an account on the [Hack The Box](https://www.hackthebox.eu/) platform.
2. **Choose Challenges:** Navigate to the challenges section and select desired challenges.
3. **Tools Needed:** Familiarity with tools such as Burp Suite, Nmap, and Metasploit.
4. **Capture the Flag:** Solutions typically involve finding a unique string of text (the flag) and submitting it back to the platform.
